### Week 1 Objectives:
* Understand and master the AWS Free Tier program, how to receive credit, and avoid unexpected costs.
* Study and practice identity management and authorization using AWS IAM (User, Group, Policy, Role, Switch Role).
* Initialize a basic VPC network infrastructure, configure firewalls (Security Group, Network ACL), and create EC2 servers.
* Practice setting up a VPN Site-to-Site connection, automate infrastructure (IaC), and master the resource cleanup process.
