**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Free Tier, IAM, VPC, VPN & Infrastructure as Code](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [EC2, RDS Integration & Auto Scaling (ALB/ASG)](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [AWS Budgets, CloudWatch Monitoring & Support Plans](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Active Directory, Hybrid DNS, AWS CLI & Advanced Permissions](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [AWS Backup, VM Import/Export & Docker Containerization](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Amazon ECS, CI/CD Pipelines & AWS Security Hub](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [VPC Peering, Transit Gateway & Serverless Automation with Lambda](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [AWS Storage Gateway, Data Deduplication & File Share Management](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [AWS WAF, Resource Tagging & Tag-based Access Control (ABAC)](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [S3 VPC Endpoints, Route 53 Hybrid DNS & S3 Bucket Policy](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Production Architecture, RDS, ECR & ECS Fargate Deployment](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [CloudWatch Container Insights, Security Hub, Cost Optimization & Project Handover](1.12-week12/)
